Project 1

Willpower

Project 1
interactive

For this artwork, I worked with a-frame as a means to make 3D artwork accessible to the mass on the world wide web. The framework is around willpower, movement, and connection, as a result of social distancing that has on a person’s mental health. It’s a true hallucinogen outside of the mind experience and puts the viewer in an unusual stage that nothing makes sense and at a loss of time and space. The pair large head whirled around in different spots in trance and confused among a sand clock placed in the middle of that is fixed in time. As you occupied there watching the scene, there are three rings come floating towards you and a line below directing you to go towards the center to a black spot. I combined the conceptual with surrealism in a very cartoonish style because there is a huge wave of new media artists on Instagram and dribbble that make this type of artwork that I fancy. For example, toothpaste compressing out a person head or a loop of a ball turning in an endless maze gif. This is the entrance and influence I take when it comes to the vision of this project combining with my narrative of social isolation and social distancing. I wanted people to perceive this artwork to say wow this is quite trippy or hallucination because my preferred type of art has been surrealism and pop art. My source of influence is Carten Holler as he plays with using mushrooms upside-down for his installation and makes the viewer interact with the artwork and the environment. I wanted mine to have a similar procedure but inside a digital atmosphere. I want people to feel shocked and lost with this art piece because the artwork communicates the concept of separation and disconnection between the mind and the physical.
